*Researching the effects of legislation on Maori land loss in the Waikato.* 
  

Role: Research Analyst & Inquiry Facilitator.  
Waikato: Te Rohe Potae District Inquiry (Wai 898).  
Waitangi Tribunal Unit, Ministry of Justice. 

Research Report:  

- Heinz, Adam, [‘Quantifying Maori Land Alienation in the Te Rohe Potae Inquiry District – Scoping Report’](https://forms.justice.govt.nz/search/Documents/WT/wt_DOC_789918/Wai%20898%2C%20A011.pdf), research report commissioned by the Waitangi Tribunal, 2008, 65pp (Wai 898 A11).  

---

Scoping the feasibility of quantifying Maori land loss in Te Rohe Potae (King Country) using historical GIS approaches to Maori land records. 
  
  
Responsibilities:  

- Develop datasets from archival records. 
- Supervise research assistants. 
- Meet research deadlines. 
- Present and defend research findings.
  

Technology:  

- ArcGIS 
- MapInfo
- MS Access   

  
Outcome:  

- Research developed an historical GIS methodology to analyse Maori land records.
