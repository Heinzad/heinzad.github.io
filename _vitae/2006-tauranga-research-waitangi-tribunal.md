*Researching the effects of Public Works legislation on the Maori land remaining in the Tauranga area after war and confiscation.* 

Role: Research Analyst & Inquiry Facilitator.  
Tauranga Moana District Inquiry Stage 2 (Wai 215).  
Waitangi Tribunal Unit, Ministry of Justice.  

Research Report:  

- Belgrave, Michael; Young, Grant; Heinz, Adam; Belgrave, David, [‘Tauranga Maori Land Alienation: A Quantitative Overview, 1886-2006’](https://forms.justice.govt.nz/search/Documents/WT/wt_DOC_93401142/Wai%20215%2C%20T016%20(a).pdf), research report commissioned by the Waitangi Tribunal, 2006, 131pp (Wai 215 T16a).  

---

Contributed a sub-project on Public Works takings of Maori land as part of a wider study of Maori land loss in Tauranga Moana.  

- Developed a methodology to analyse digitised notices of public works takings in the NZ Gazette. 
- Recruited and supervised a team of research assistants for data entry. 
- Contributed cleansed data to the main project and wrote a section on methodology for the report. 
- Defended research findings on public works under cross-examination. 
- Research cited extensively in the report by the Waitangi Tribunal, Tauranga Moana, 1886-2006. 


Responsibilities:  

- Develop datasets from archival records. 
- Supervise research assistants. 
- Meet research deadlines.  
- Present and defend research findings.
